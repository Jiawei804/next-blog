Introduction

I am currently a master's student at North China Electric Power University, studying under Professor Li Yuancheng and following Dr. Li Changlin in the field of artificial intelligence. Li Changlin is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the ReLER Laboratory of the Australian Institute of Artificial Intelligence. I am currently researching image and video generation, neural network search and automatic progressive learning.

我目前是华北电力大学硕士生，师从李元诚教授，跟随李长林博士研究人工智能领域，李长林目前是澳大利亚人工智能研究所 ReLER 实验室的博士后研究员。我目前在研究图像与视频生成，神经网络搜索和自动渐进学习。
